About Us

The goal of this project is to develop a platform that allows YFRB token holders to utilize flash loans from platforms like AAVE, dYdX, etc. to engage in arbitrage opportunities on multiple decentralized exchanges. Flash loans are a unique loan option that allows for users to borrow funds without collateral as long as the amount borrowed is repaid in the same Ethereum transaction. Flash loans are not easily accessible to the average crypto investor without a strong technical background or understanding of the inner workings of smart contracts. YFRB aims to bridge this knowledge gap by providing tools that allow even average investors to find potential profitable arbitrage opportunities.
